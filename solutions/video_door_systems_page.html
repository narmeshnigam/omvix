VDP vs. VDB Explained

Video Door Phone (VDP)

  • Wall-mounted indoor screen
  • Live video + intercom
  • Best for in-home families
  • Wired installation

Video Door Bell (VDB)

  • App-based control from anywhere
  • Motion detection + video alerts
  • Best for remote monitoring and renters
  • Wi-Fi based or hybrid
